How To Play

ðŸ¤– Hello, my name is Doodle Data - I analyze your drawings and try to guess them. Help me become a smarter robot!

Doodle Data is a local two-player game in which you and a friend are given a prompt, and have 60 seconds each to draw the prompt to the best of your abilities. After 10 turns, your drawings are analyzed by an AI - if the AI recognizes your drawing as the given prompt, you get a point! The person with the most points wins.

  1. This AI is not smart. Draw to the best of your abilities!
  2. Each player has the same prompt for a given turn.
  3. Players turns are rotated immediately; make sure you're ready to go!
  4. Scores are only displayed at the end. No cheating!
